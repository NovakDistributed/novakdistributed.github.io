Novak Distributed

Novak Distributed is an independent distributed systems company. Our specialties include:

  • End-to-end solutions for counter-anti-disintermediation
  • Blockchain technologies for non-financial applications
  • Creative use of strong cryptographic primitives
  • Social construct design and implementation

Our most well-known project is Macroverse, a procedurally-generated galaxy on the Ethereum blockchain.

We also maintain Brutestrap, a brutalist web design framework.
